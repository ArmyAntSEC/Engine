This is how the program is structured. Use it to see what classes are important, and as a quick overview.
  • Engine, the main applet.
    • View, the view-port.
    • Buttons, the input.
    • Info, status info.
    • Help, some instructions.
    • Framer, handles redrawing.
    • Clock, handles physical time.
    • Scene, the world.
      • Dummy, a dummy.
      • Sphere, round.
      • Part, moves around.
        • Throws: CollisionEvent
      • Star, dots in the distance.
      • Tripod, a coordinate system.
      • Camera, everyone needs a viewpoint.
